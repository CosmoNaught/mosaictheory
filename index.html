Who we are

The intelligence layer between research and markets.

Mosaic Theory was founded on a simple observation: the signals that precede major market moves are public. Research breakthroughs, infrastructure acquisitions, talent migrations, policy shifts. They're just scattered across thousands of sources, published in different formats, at different times, in different communities.


We build the systems that detect, weight, and converge these signals into a single, actionable intelligence layer. Hedge funds and venture capital firms use Mosaic Theory to identify asymmetric opportunities before consensus forms.
